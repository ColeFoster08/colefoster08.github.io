Usage of Cabinets Over Time

In the past conventional cabinets were mainly used for storing papers and clothes, but now they have become so much more and they are now one of the main pieces of furniture in any room in any house. And over time cabinets started to evolve, change their general look and become more convenient for the average user.

Also cabinets can now be put basically anywhere and are universally recognized as the main method of storage in a household.
