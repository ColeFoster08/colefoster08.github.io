History of Cabinets

In 50,000 BC caveman's used the earlies form of cabinets, caves, which they used to store their items. Then in 3150 BC food containers start to wide spread and this could be considered a small mobile cabinet. Next in 800 BC the cabinet evolved into storing clothes which begin it evolution into what it is toady. After that cabinets started to take over the world and around 1850 the cabinet spread to the US where it was widley accepted. Then in 1886 and 1898 the filing cabinets were invented (both horizontal and vertical)

Because of these advacements made over time, cabinets were able to evole to what they are today and they are used all over the world to store many different kinds of things
